# 归元·留白 — App Store 应用描述

## 中文版

### 标题
**归元·留白 — 数字时代的静心工具箱**

### 副标题
回归本元。一分钟也好。

### 正文

**在喧嚣的世界里，给自己留一片空白。**

归元·留白不是一个普通的效率工具。它是一个帮你主动与数字世界断联、回归内心本元的工具箱。纯黑底色，苔绿光晕，每一次打开都是一次呼吸。

#### 四大修习

**⚓ 此在（Be Here）**
一分钟冥想，归元于身。跟随随机引导问题，把注意力从思绪拉回呼吸。倒计时结束，你在此地。此身。此刻。

**↻ 屏息（Pause）**
选择一段安静的时间，让手机暂时变回一块板砖。倒计时期间自动保持屏幕常亮、静音设备——你与世界断联的每一分钟，都在重新学会呼吸。

**⊙ 落痕（Trace）**
每日一摄。不修图，不删改，不积压。拍下今天的瞬间，配一句话，让时间有迹可循。

**✉ 云中笺（Cloud Letter）**
写一封信，指定一位收信人——可以是他人，也可以是自己。一天之后，信件「送达」。慢下来，说值得被等待的话。

#### 时间线
所有修习自动记录。冥想、屏息、照片、信件——每一条操作按时间排列，照片的缩略图嵌入时间节点，形成你的归元日记。

#### 设计哲学

- **纯黑底色** — 每一次打开，都是无限虚空中的一次归元
- **不留痕迹** — 无账户、无社交、无排行榜。只属于你一个人的数字留白
- **中英双语** — 完整支持中文和英文，切换自如
- **离线可用** — 所有功能无需网络连接

#### 定价

一次性购买，无订阅，无内购。

## English Version

### Title
**Guiyuan · Liubai — A Digital Mindfulness Toolkit**

### Subtitle
Return to origin. Even if just for a minute.

### Description

**A quiet place in your pocket.**

Guiyuan · Liubai is not another productivity app. It's a toolkit for voluntarily disconnecting from the digital noise and returning to your origin. Pure black canvas, moss-green glow — every opening is a breath.

#### Four Practices

**⚓ Be Here**
One minute of meditation. Settle into your body. Follow the gentle prompts as they guide your attention back from thought to breath. When the timer ends — you're here. This body. This moment.

**↻ Pause**
Choose a stretch of silence. Let your phone become a brick again. During the countdown, the screen stays awake, the device goes quiet. Every minute you disconnect from the world, you remember how to breathe.

**⊙ Trace**
One frame a day. No filters. No deletions. No backlog. Capture today's moment, add a single sentence, and let time leave a trail.

**✉ Cloud Letter**
Write a letter. Name your recipient — someone else, or yourself. One day later, it arrives. Slow words from the heart. Tomorrow. Not a second sooner.

#### Timeline
Every practice is recorded. Meditations, pauses, photos, letters — each entry appears chronologically. Photo thumbnails are embedded as timeline nodes, forming your personal return-to-origin journal.

#### Design Philosophy

- **Pure black canvas** — Every launch is a return to the void, a fresh beginning
- **No trace left behind** — No accounts, no social features, no leaderboards. Your private digital sanctuary
- **Bilingual** — Full support for both Chinese and English
- **Works offline** — Everything runs locally, no internet required

#### Pricing

One-time purchase. No subscription. No in-app purchases.

## 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

**更新日期 / Last updated: 2026-05-14**

### 中文

归元·留白（以下简称"本应用"）尊重并保护您的隐私。

#### 我们收集的信息

**本应用不收集任何个人数据。**

- 无需注册账户，无需登录
- 不收集姓名、邮箱、位置、设备标识符等个人信息
- 不追踪您的使用行为
- 不接入任何第三方分析服务（如 Google Analytics、Firebase 等）
- 不展示广告
- 不含任何社交分享功能

#### 数据存储

您在使用本应用过程中产生的所有数据（冥想记录、屏息记录、照片、信件内容等）**仅存储在您的设备本地**。我们无法访问这些数据。

如果您卸载本应用或清除应用数据，这些记录将永久丢失。建议定期通过时间线的导出功能备份数据。

#### 相机与相册权限

本应用仅在您使用「落痕」功能拍摄每日照片时，请求访问您的相机和相册。拍摄的照片仅保存在您的设备本地，不会被上传或分享。

您可以随时在系统设置中开启或关闭这些权限。

#### 第三方服务

本应用不集成任何第三方 SDK 或服务。

#### 隐私政策更新

如果我们对本隐私政策做出任何更改，将在本页面更新。继续使用本应用即表示您同意更新后的条款。

#### 联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请联系：guiyuan.liubai@outlook.com

### English

Guiyuan · Liubai (the "App") respects and protects your privacy.

#### What We Collect

**This App does not collect any personal data.**

- No account registration or login required
- No collection of name, email, location, device identifiers, or any personal information
- No usage tracking
- No third-party analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- No advertisements
- No social sharing features

#### Data Storage

All data generated while using the App (meditation records, pause sessions, photos, letter contents, etc.) is **stored locally on your device only**. We have no access to this data.

If you uninstall the App or clear its data, these records will be permanently lost. It is recommended to use the timeline export feature periodically for backup.

#### Camera & Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your camera and photo library only when you use the "Trace" feature to capture your daily photo. Photos are saved locally on your device only and are never uploaded or shared.

You may grant or revoke these permissions at any time in your system settings.

#### Third-Party Services

This App does not integrate any third-party SDKs or services.

#### Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page. Your continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

For privacy-related inquiries: guiyuan.liubai@outlook.com
